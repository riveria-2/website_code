Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work with my Shopify theme? ▼
Yes! Riveria apps are built using Shopify's Checkout Extensibility, which means they work natively with all Shopify themes and don't require any theme code injection.
Will this slow down my checkout? ▼
Not at all. Because we use native Shopify components, our apps load instantly and have zero impact on your checkout speed or performance.
Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all paid plans. We also have a "Forever Free" plan for stores just starting out.
Do I need a developer to set this up? ▼
No developer needed. You can install and configure everything yourself in under 5 minutes using our intuitive drag-and-drop builder.
What Shopify plans does this work with? ▼
Our apps work with all Shopify plans, including Basic, Shopify, Advanced, and Shopify Plus.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription directly from your Shopify admin at any time.
